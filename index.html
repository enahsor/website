About Me

I'm Dowen Robinson, software developer, graphic designer and data analyst based in Kingston, Jamaica. I specialize in web development and graphic designing. I create robust and easy-to-maintain software using industry best practices. For software development, my weapons of choice are Python and Javascript.

If I'm not sitting around my computer, I'm out for a run or annoying my girlfriend. I love me some good music and cheesecake 🌝
